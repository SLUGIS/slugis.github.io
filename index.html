WELCOME TO CAL FIRE SLUGIS

CAL FIRE SLUGIS


The CAL FIRE SLUGIS (San Luis Unit GIS) is a division of CAL FIRE SLU that aims to reduce property losses, reduce fire fighting costs, and increase firefighter safety long before a fire starts. SLUGIS exists thanks to funding from SLO County as well as the San Luis Obispo County Fire Safe Council.
